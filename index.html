About Me

I’m a self-taught nail artist who loves intricate designs, chrome finishes, and everything aesthetic. Based in the NJ area, I turn your hands into a literal mood board. My name is Rishika Desai, and I am a 9th-grade student at South Brunswick High School. My journey into nail artistry began as a creative outlet, but it quickly evolved into a dedicated entrepreneurial pursuit. I don't just see nail design as aesthetic; I see it as a blend of chemistry, precision, and customer communication. By mastering techniques like acrylic application and intricate design work, I’ve learned the importance of detail and technical consistency. Beyond the art, I manage the 'business' side of my practice—from inventory management and cost analysis to scheduling and client relations. This platform serves as a digital record of my growth, showcasing not only the evolution of my designs but also the development of the professional habits that define my work ethic
